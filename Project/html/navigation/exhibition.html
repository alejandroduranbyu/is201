A Taxonomy of Smiles

Not all smiles are created equal. Here is a brief curatorial guide:

  1. The Classic Smile
    • Symmetrical, warm, universally recognized
    • The :) of the real world
  2. The Smirk
    • One-sided, knowing, slightly dangerous
    • Reserved for those who are in on the joke
  3. The Grin
    • Wide, toothy, uninhibited
    • The :D in its natural habitat
  4. The Smiley Face
    • Abstracted from humanity entirely
    • The subject of this very exhibition
    • Yellow, circular, eternal
